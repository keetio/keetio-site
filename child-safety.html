Keetio – Child Safety & Reporting Policy

Zero tolerance for child exploitation, abuse, grooming, or harassment of minors.

Who this statement is for

This page explains Keetio’s safety standards regarding minors and our process for handling child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, or any attempt to endanger a minor.

Keetio is intended for adults. Content or behavior that targets, exploits, endangers, or sexually approaches minors is strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate action.

Zero tolerance policy

Keetio has zero tolerance for:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Sexualization of minors in any form.
  • Grooming, coercion, or approaching a minor for sexual purposes.
  • Harassment, threats, or exploitation of minors.

Any user suspected of sharing, requesting, or attempting to obtain CSAM, or of attempting inappropriate contact with a minor, will be suspended or permanently banned. Content will be removed.

Reporting unsafe or illegal behavior

Users can report inappropriate behavior, harassment, or safety concerns directly from within the app using the in-app report / safety tools. You may also contact us directly at:

Email: safety@keetio.com

We review reports as quickly as possible. Confirmed violations will result in content removal and account action, which may include permanent bans.

Enforcement and cooperation with authorities

Keetio complies with applicable child safety laws. Where required by law, we will escalate confirmed CSAM or credible threats to child safety to the appropriate authorities.

We will cooperate with law enforcement consistent with legal obligations to protect minors.

Your responsibility

If you believe a user is attempting to exploit, harm, or contact a minor, or if you encounter content that appears to involve a minor sexually, you must report it immediately using the in-app report option or by emailing safety@keetio.com. Please include as much detail as possible.

Do not distribute or forward any suspected CSAM. Send a report; our team will handle it according to policy and legal requirements.

Our commitment

• We remove content and accounts that violate these rules.
• We act quickly on credible reports.
• We will escalate to authorities where required.
• We continuously work to keep Keetio safe.

Safety is not optional. It is a condition of using Keetio.
