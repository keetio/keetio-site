Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how Keetio handles your data:

  • We do not collect personal data unless explicitly provided (e.g., name, profile info).
  • Location is used only for matching nearby users and never stored permanently.
  • Profile pictures are stored locally on your device only.
  • Messages are stored on-device and not shared with third parties.
  • We use no advertising or tracking cookies.

If you have questions, contact support@keetio.app
