How Keetio Works

1. Explore Nearby People

When you open Keetio, you land on the Explore tab, where you can discover people around you in two ways:

Map Mode: Nearby users appear as pins on the map. Their position is slightly shifted (about 300–500 meters) to protect their exact location. Tap a pin to view their profile and send a Greeting.

WiFi Mode: If you're connected to the same WiFi network as others (for example in a bar, café, or event), you’ll see a list of users instead of a map. No location is shown. Tap a name to view their profile and send a Greeting.

2. Send a Greeting

A Greeting is the first step to connect. It’s a simple and respectful way to show interest. You can send a Greeting by tapping a pin (Map Mode) or a name (WiFi Mode).

If the other person accepts your Greeting, a private chat opens instantly for both of you. Messaging is only possible after mutual acceptance.

3. Accept or Ignore

When you receive a Greeting, you decide what happens next:

Accept: A chat opens and you can start talking immediately.

Ignore: Nothing happens — no notifications, no pressure.

You are always in full control of who you interact with.

4. Chat & Interaction

Once a Greeting is accepted, a chat is created automatically. You can send messages and get to know each other freely.

From both the user profile and the chat screen, you can block or report any user at any time.

5. Your Profile (User Tab)

In the User tab, you can set up your profile with your photo, name, age, and a short bio. You can also add an optional description to help others recognize you or understand your situation or location.

6. Privacy & Safety

Keetio is designed with privacy in mind. Your exact location is never shown, and map positions are always offset. In WiFi Mode, no location is displayed at all.

You control when you appear and who you interact with, and you can block or report users at any time.

7. Greetings & Subscription

Every user can send 2 free Greetings per day. With a subscription, you can send unlimited Greetings and connect without limits.

8. Languages

Keetio currently supports 12 languages, with more coming in future updates.
