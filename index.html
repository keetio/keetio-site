A Social App for Real-World Spaces

Keetio is a location-based presence app that helps people connect when they’re in the same area or venue. Using a dynamic map or shared WiFi signals, users appear as avatars and can send greetings to nearby users. If both sides agree, a private chat opens — safe, friendly, and controlled by you.

Two Modes. One Simple Goal: Connection.

  • Map Mode: See other users on a live map with intentional location offset for privacy (e.g. 300–500 meters). Great for spotting nearby connections in parks, cities, campuses, or streets.
  • WiFi Mode: Perfect for indoor spaces like cafés, clubs, or events. When you're on the same WiFi, you see a list of others nearby — no map needed. Just name, age, and vibe.

Say Hi — the keetio Way

Forget random DMs or creepy inboxes. On keetio, you start with a simple Greeting. It’s like a wave — if they wave back, you can chat. If not, no awkwardness. No one can message you unless you say yes. It’s mutual, smooth, and respectful.

Privacy Comes First

Keetio is not a dating app. It's a social presence tool built with safety in mind:

  • ✅ No live tracking
  • ✅ No spam inbox
  • ✅ No pressure to reveal your name or photo

You're always in control. Appear when you want. Vanish when you don’t.
