Connect in real time

See Who’s Nearby, Right Now.

Keetio works in real time, helping you discover people who are close to you at that moment — in the same area, the same venue, or the same WiFi network.

It is built for spontaneous, real-world connection: simple, natural, and made for the moment you are actually there.

Map Mode + WiFi Mode

Two Modes. One Simple Goal: Connection.

  • Map Mode: Discover nearby people through Google Maps, with a privacy-preserving location offset of about 300–500 meters.
  • WiFi Mode: See people who are in the same space and connected to the same WiFi network, shown in a simple list — no map needed.
Made for real places

A Social App for Real-World Spaces.

Keetio is made for places where people naturally gather: campuses, cafés, parks, events, music venues, and busy city spots.

Instead of endless scrolling, Keetio focuses on real presence — people who are actually nearby and open to connect.

The Keetio Greeting

Say Hi — the Keetio Way.

Communication starts with a simple Greeting. If the other person accepts, a private chat opens. If not, nothing else happens — no awkwardness, no pressure, and no unwanted inbox.

It feels more like a real-life wave than a random message: mutual, respectful, and controlled by both sides.

Privacy-first

No Exact Location. No Unwanted Messages.

Keetio does not show your exact location. In Map Mode, your position is protected with an approximate offset of about 300–500 meters, so people can understand that you are nearby without seeing exactly where you are.

  • ✅ No exact live location
  • ✅ No message unless both sides agree
  • ✅ Appear when you want, disappear when you don’t
